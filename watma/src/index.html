Travellers Journals

By Ellen Olenska

Devon Tours recently took two dozen students of Tiverton High School to Chichen Itza, Mexico. While on the tour, students saw ancient Mayan ruins and artifacts. They learned the latest on the "mystery of the Mayans."

The Mayans were one of the world's most advanced civilizations when they mysteriously disappeared centuries ago. While archaeologists don't know exactly what happened to the Mayans, evidence points to war as one possible explanation. Tiverton High School students are using the experience as the basis for a series of reports that teams of participants are putting together.
