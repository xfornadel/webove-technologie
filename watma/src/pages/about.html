About Devon Tours

Archer and May Smith, hiking enthusiasts with a taste for exotic culture and scenery, founded Devon Tours in 1964 to offer travel tours for a variety of interests and activity levels.

In the late 1960s, Devon Tours specialized exclusively in travel tours for those leading active lifestyles, which were usually led by Archer or May themselves. While many of our tours still emphasize activity—including hiking, kayaking, inline skating, and bicycling—most of our tours are suitable for the whole family.

Today, our tours are still led by experienced traveling enthusiasts, whose knowledge of the locations is outpaced only by their passion for sharing it.
